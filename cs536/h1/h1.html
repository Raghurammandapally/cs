  1. Draw a deterministic finite state machine that accepts the language of email addresses. An email address:

    1. can include letters, digits, underscores ( _ ), and periods ( . )

    2. must start with a letter or underscore

    3. must include an @ symbol

    4. must include at least two or more letters or digits before the @ sign

    5. cannot have an underscore after the @ sign

    6. must include at least one letter or digit after the @ sign

      Label the edges of your FSM with the characters _ and . (or use the word dot) and the words letter and digit.

  2. Write a regular expression for the language of identifiers. An identifier:

    1. can include dashes ( - ) and letters

    2. cannot have more than three dashes in a row

    3. must include at least one letter (i.e., one, two, or three dashes alone are not legal identifiers)

You may use letter to mean any letter.
